About Me

My Introduction

I am a passionate Designer with experience in wireframing, UI patterns, and user flows. For the past 2 years, I am currently working as UI UX Designer and Full Stack Developer (.Net) at Softlogic Life Insurance. During my first year, I worked with Asp .Net, Microsoft SQL, SSRS, C #, etc., and I realized my creativity and trained myself to be a UI UX Engineer at that company.

Since then, I have served as a UI UX engineer on several of Softlogic Life's major systems. I am currently using Adobe's creative tools (XD, Photoshop, Illustrator) and Zeplin for Designing and Prototype and Familiar with Angular 12, Material design bootstrap, HTML, SCSS, TS for Development. I was always eager to increase my knowledge and creativity by gradually learning them.
